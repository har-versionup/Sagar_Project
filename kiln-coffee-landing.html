Our Philosophy

Slow work, on purpose.

Kiln started in a foundry building that once cast iron radiators. We kept the exposed brick, the tall windows, and something of the patience the old trade required. Roasting coffee, like casting metal, rewards people who wait for the material to tell them it's done.

We buy in small lots, direct from four cooperatives we visit every year, and we roast just enough for the week ahead. Nothing sits in a warehouse. Nothing is roasted twice as fast to hit a number.
